Thai Green Curry Chicken

Ingredients

  • Thai basil
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green curry paste
  • Full fat coconut milk
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Chicken stock

Preparation

  1. Pound green curry paste and Thai basil leaves for an extra vibrant colour.
  2. Get the paste as fine as possible.
  3. Reduce the coconut milk until very thick
  4. Add curry paste and saute for a few minutes
  5. Toss chicken with curry paste
  6. Add coconut milk
  7. Add chicken stock
  8. Add fish sauce and palm sugar
  9. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fork tender
  10. Add bamboo shoots and cook for a minute until the curry comes back to a boil
  11. Stir in red bell pepper and turn off the heat
  12. Add Thai basil and serve with jasmine rice
